ROBOGALS

Volunteering, Teaching, Networking, Socialising

Membership Fees: $2

We encourage school aged girls to pursue Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM) through holding workshops at schools, universities and libraries because diversity drives innovation.

We have a friendly, warm and genuine culture which provides support for club members. Robogals offers opportunities to develop skills such as public speaking and communication skills. Additionally, we connect university students to companies that value a gender inclusive workplace.

All faculties are welcome as we provide training.

Throughout the year, Robogals holds numerous events including: (all events are not age restrictive)

  • Launch nights welcoming volunteers with pizza and board games.
  • Volunteer-run workshops for school students
  • Rural trips where volunteers travel to rural Queensland for a week to inspire young minds. Past locations have included: Sunshine Coast, Gladstone and Townsville*
  • An Industry Gala to connect university students to companies that value a gender inclusive workplace
  • Social events: from having lunch to bowling and laser tag!

*Rural trips are free for Robogals members
